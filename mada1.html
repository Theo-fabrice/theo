East Tours

The East of Madagascar is very rich in fauna and flora because it is where Madagascar has its rainforest.
The rainforest of Madagascar is home to diverse species of Lemurs, weird chameleons; Leaf tailed Geckos, several endemic bird species, orchids and succulents…
The Masoala National Park, Andasibe-Mantadia National Parks and Ranomafana National Park are the highlights for the amazing wildlife of Madagascar: The indri-indri known as the biggest Lemur specie in Madagascar, the Diademad Sifakas which is the most beautiful tricolored Sifaka, the Leaf tailed Geckos called Uroplatus Fimbriatus, the biggest Parson’s Chameleon..


We suggest 4 different tours in the East coast:
1. INDRI TOUR: 3 DAY TOUR
Circuit: Tana – Andasibe National Park – Tana
2. GREEN TOUR: 4 DAY TOUR
Circuit: Tana - Andasibe National Park - Mahambo Beach - Soanierana Ivongo - Boat to Sainte Marie Island.
3. NEST OF DREAM TOUR: 5 DAY TOUR
Circuit: Tana - Andasibe National Park - Ferry to Private Lemur’s Park in Ankanin’ny Nofy - Ferry to Tamatave- Drive to Soanierana Ivongo and Ferry to Sainte Marie Island.
4. ZAHAMENA TOUR: 6 DAY TOUR
Circuit: Tana – Ambatondrazaka – Andreba – Trek and camping in the Zahamena National Park – Tana
