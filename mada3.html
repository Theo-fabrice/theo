West Tours

The West of Madagascar is famous for its Baobab trees. The Tsingy de Bemaraha or the limestones pinnacles formation is among the Best National Park to visit in Madagascar.
The Tsiribihina River tour with dugout canoe is among the best camping trip in Madagascar. The wild Manambolo River is very spectacular place

1-The Tsiribihina river trip: 6 to 7 day tour
Circuit: Tana - Antsirabe - 2 or 3 days on the river – Visit of Baobabs – Morondava.
2- The Tsingy of Bemaraha (limestone pinnacles): 4 to 5 day tour
Circuit: Fly from Tana the (center) or from Tulear (the South) to Morondava – 4WD to the Tsingy of Bemaraha – 2 days exploration of the National park and World Wide Heritage including visit with a dugout canoe in the gorge of the Manambolo river – 4WD back to Kirindy forest (the best place for nocturnal Lemurs, mouse Lemurs) – visit of the Baobabs trees– Morondava.
3- Mixed Tsiribihina River and Tsingy tour: 9 day tour
This combines the canoe trip down the Tsiribihina River with the hike into the Tsingy de Bemaraha National Park. After the river, drive to the Tsingy of Bemaraha for 2 days exploration of the National Park, drive to visit baobab trees including the famous Baobab Avenue, then driv3 to Morondava.
