North Tours

Circuit: Tana – Ankarafantsika National Park – Tsingy d’Ankarana Special Reserve – Amber Mountain National park – Diego Suarez – Ramena Beach – Ankify – Nosy Be Island
The visit starts a day after arrival or on arrival at the international airport.

Day 1: Drive from Tana to Ankarafantsika National Park
Start from Tana, drive to Ankarafantsika National Park, Overnight in a Hotel or Camping
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Ankarafantsika] The Lodge runs by the National Park
Day 2: Visit of Ankarafantsika National Park
Full day visit of Ankarafantsika National Park: Lemurs, Birds, reptiles…, go on a night walk to see the mouse lemurs and other nocturnal species of wildlife, Overnight in a Hotel or Camping
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Ankarafantsika] The Lodge runs by the National Park
Day 3: Drive from Ankarafantsika to Antsohihy
Drive from Ankarafantsika to Antsohihy: Landscape, people and village, Overnight in a Hotel
Day 4: Drive from Antsohihy to the Tsingy of Ankarana National Park, Overnight in a Hotel
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Antsohihy] Relais des Sofia: Simple accommodation
Day 5: Visit of Ankarana National Park
Full day visit of the Ankarana National Park: Limestones pinnacles, caves, landscape, Overnight in a Hotel
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Ankarana] Ankarana Lodge: Basic hotel
Day 6: Visit of the Tsingy Rouge de Diego Suarez
Drive from Ankarana National Park to visit the Tsingy Rouge in Diego Suarez: Eroded red soil called Tsingy rouge or Red Tsingy: spectacular
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Diego Suarez] La Rascasse Hotel (mid range), Concorde Hotel (mid range), In Ramena: Ramena Nofy Hotel (mid range)
Day 7: Visit of Amber Mountain National Park
This is a full day visit to see: Lemurs, Sifakas (white Lemurs), Birds including the Amber Mountain Rock Thrush as a local endemic bird), frogs, chameleons, waterfall...
Drive back to Diego Suarez, Overnight in a Hotel
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Diego Suarez] La Rascasse Hotel (mid range), Concorde Hotel (mid range), In Ramena: Ramena Nofy Hotel (mid range)
Day 8: Visit of the 3 baies de Diego Suarez
Drive from Diego to visit the 3 BAIES: Pigeon, Sakalava and Dune: Beautiful landscapes, wildlife
Extension to Ramena Beach, Overnight in a Hotel in Ramena Beach Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Ramena Beach] Ramena Nofy Hotel (mid range)
Day 9: Drive from Diego Suarez to Ambanja to reach Nosy Be Island
Drive from Ramena Beach to Diego then from Diego Suarez to Ambanja, Overnight in a Hotel in Ambanja.
Hotel: [Recommended Hotel in Ambanja] Hotel Palma Nova (Luxury with A.C), Hotel Patricia (mid range)
Day 10: Drive from Ambanja to Ankify where ferry boats leave to Nosy Be Island.
Our service ends here when you are on the ferry boat to go to Nosy Be Island. You choose how long you would like to stay to explore Nosy Be and the small Islands in the surrounding of Nosy Be but you should have your internal flight to leave Nosy Be at the end of your stay there.
