Biking

Tritriva Lake

From 9 am, Bicycle ride from your Hotel to the Lake Tritriva, picnic lunch above the volcanic Lake Tritriva: beautiful landscape and countryside, unpaved road. In the afternoon, bicycle ride to the natural hot spring which is on the road to Betafo. Follow the tarmac on the National Road number 34 to ride back to Antsirabe, - Overnight in the Hotel.

At 9 am from the hotel, ride through the city and to the Hill of Ambohimanga: beautiful landscape and 360° view on Antsirabe and its vicinities: the city of Antsirabe, the countryside, picnic lunch on the Hill of Ambohijatovo. In the afternoon, bicycle ride through unpaved road in the countryside to the waterfall of Ambohijatovo. Ride to the Hill of Ivohitra for sunset photography, another 180° view on the city of Antsirabe, ride back to the Hotel, and overnight at the same Hotel. End of the bicycle tour
